Fairytales by Josh Knight

Knight's paintings are unique. He brings to life the colours of the world with wild and riotous movement but, the chaos on the canvas does not reflect the meticulous and inventive process that allowed him to capture bodies in motion. After the talk Josh will be able to answer your questions in a live Q&A.

About the Artist

Josh Knight, an artist from Basel, is one of European's most admired landscape painters. His evocative impressionist paintings with significantly reduced details masterfully walk the fine line between impact and subtlety. Josh Knight's artistic purpose of documenting his impressions of the European landscape and reverence for nature keeps him exploring the backroads of the continent in search of the next motif.
