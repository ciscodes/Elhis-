Our History

ELHIS gallery has been in Basel connecting wonderful people with European's best visual artists for more than 20 years. We take great pride in working with such talented people and great pleasure in hanging artworks in homes and offices that will be admired and appreciated for generations to come. There is no better, more affordable way in which to beautify and enrich your home and your life than with a well selected piece of art. While there is certainly a division of category and genre between American and European style painting , that division is somewhat unwarranted from a curatorial sense in our art gallery. We present Australia's best artists, regardless of genre and strive to showcase the very best examples of their work.
